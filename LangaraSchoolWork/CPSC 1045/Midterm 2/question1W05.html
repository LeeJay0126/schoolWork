For this part of the midterm, you will have write a short program to check if a secret message has been encoded correctly in a larger block of text

The cipher works as follows. It encodes a letter of the secret a word every nth word of a sentence at the jth letter. Each sentence encodes 1 word. Sentence ends with the punctuation "."

Example:

For the secret message "hi mom", with n=2, and j=1 the following message would be valid

"The hidden small invertebrate hides. It meanders slowly on the mountain pass."

For the secret message "it is nice", with n=3, and j=2 the following message would be valid

"Moms is digging for an italian doubloon. She has nine holes and is not stopping. The neighbors snarky remarks are sinister, mean and scathing but not pertinent."
