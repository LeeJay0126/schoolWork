Archery Simulator

For this program there is an number box. When the user changes the number your program should simulate that many arrows being shot at the target. The simulation will need to do two things. It will use the use tag and the group#arrows to place arrows into image, and it will calculate the score.
For every arrow whose centers land in the red circle they get 20 points.
For every arrow whose centers land in the green ring they get 15 points.
For every arrow whose centers land in the white ring they get 10 points.
For every arrows whose centers land in the blue circle they get 5 points.
All other arrows get 0 points.

You will need to use pythagoras equation to calculate the distance from the center of the target to the center of the circle. tutorial
