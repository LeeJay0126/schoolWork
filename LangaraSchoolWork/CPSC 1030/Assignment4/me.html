The Sun

I like the Sun. I don't know much science behind the sun, but I like sunshine and warmth it gives. I love warm summer and summer activities. Warm, sunny day makes my mood hype up and even just staying in my room knowing the day is sunny makes my day.

I face profound depression during the winter because there rarely ever is a sunny day here in vancouver during the season. I bet 95% of world population prefer sun over rain. Anyday.
