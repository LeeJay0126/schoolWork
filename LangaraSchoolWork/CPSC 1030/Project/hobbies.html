My hobbies

My Life outside of Work

What people often call a work-life balance, is very important because it keeps you revitalized and give you the energy to work harder at workplace. I would like to share some of mine to show you what I am interested in.

My hobbie is to play video games and I love basketball. I play a bit of video games when I do get a chance and keep on track with NBA games with youtube. Going out with my girlfriend or taking some time off work, watching basketball games or playing games really help me cope with stress from my everyday life and it allows me to be more efficient when I need to be working hard. My Favorite basketball team is, needless to say, Toronto Raptors, and my favorite player is Demar Derozan. I may not be the best basketball player / video gamer in the world, but I believe these hobbies bring more pros than cons to the table for me.

Food

I also love food. It just wasn't my thing, but it recently became one of the things I absolutely love. I love to look up a specific recipe that I have in mind, and try it at home. I also love going to new restaurants and try out different culture and their food. Nowadays I am in to fine dining after months of trying different exotic foods from all over the globe. Going to a new restaurant is something that I look forward to and is a motivation when I'm at work.


Hobbies are like my hideout in life. Whenever I feel stressed or need to catch a breath, I often focus on my hobbies. By doing what I like, it distracts me from the cause of stress. It also plays its' role as an ice breaker to start up a conversation!
