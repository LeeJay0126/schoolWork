My Favorite Places to Travel

Korea

Although I don't get to spend much time in Korea, it is my favorite place to go to for a vacation. Everything is very nostalgic from when I left the country at age of 7. Korea is very advanced and highly populated just like any other east-Asian countries. They have amazing delivery system and public transportation organized.

Venice

Me and my brother went to a month long trip to Europe last winter. My favorite place in Europe was Venice. They had a very unique structure of the city and their main transportation was by boat. I loved the food there as well. I wish to go there once more when I get the chance to.

France

Last winter, my experience in France was very nice. It was full of events and I enjoyed every minute of it. While Venice was the most pleasant place I have been in Europe, France definitely was the most memorable. I am definitely going back to France with more preperation next time.

Harrison Hotspring

One of my favorite local travel place. Place is just relaxing and chill with few things that could ever bother you. I recommend Harrison Hotsprings if you are looking for a place to rest and take a day off.

Whistler

As a person who LOVES snowboarding, whistler has to be one of my favorite travel place to go during the winter. Great runs with great views can never go wrong. It is not too far from Vancouver and can be relatively cheap compared to travel overseas.
