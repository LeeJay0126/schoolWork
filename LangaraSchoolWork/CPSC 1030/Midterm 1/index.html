Knife Safety

Do not!

Knives are useful, but can cause injury.

Here is a list of dangerous things that you should never do with a knife!

  1. Do not brush your teeth with a knife
  2. Do not throw it at the wall
    • Even if your roommate is being annoying
  3. Do not stick it in an electrical socket

Do!

Here are some tips to help you get a lot of usage out of your knife.

Follow these tips to stay safe.

  1. Do store your knife in a secure place where children cannot get to it
  2. Do maintain your knife, including regular sharpening

Compare! Knives vs pencils

Many people are confused about the difference between knives and pencils.

Here's a handy comparison

  • Knives are good for cutting things
  • Knives are very sharp
  • Pencils are good for writing stuff
  • Pencils are only a little big sharp
